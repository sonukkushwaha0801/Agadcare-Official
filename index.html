Agad Care


On 11th January 2020, that day was quite terrifying for me. It was around 10 in the morning when I was coming back from coaching when I had a road accident. At that time, I couldn't understand anything, I blacked out. I was in so much pain that I can't forget till today. Some people took me to the nearest private hospital and they refused to give me first aid, the reason being that I didn't have any family member with me to pay the bill.

What happened to me happens daily with thousands of people, about 1 lakh accidents are reported per day, in which 43% of people die because government hospitals are far away and no one goes to a private hospital due to high bills, which causes delay in treatment leading to their death or worsening condition just because of a little delay. So, I came up with a solution to this problem, Agad card, through which the patient will receive first aid amount in the hospital's account when the funds are needed.

Getting an Agad card is very simple, a person just needs to go to the Agad Care website and enter some credentials to get an Agad ID issued. Then, they can call the hospital anytime with this ID so that when fund needed, the hospital will receive the amount from Agad Care. And this amount will be deducted from the customer's account at the rate of some rupees per month and will be credited to their Agad ID, which will be used for their first aid
